A summer full of smiles starts here ☀️

Designed especially for young kiddos ages 2–6, LOL Summer Camp packs every day with laughs, love, and even a little bit of learning🤫. From June 16 to August 15, we meet Monday–Friday, 9 AM – 1 PM right here in Sterling, VA. Each week brings a new and exciting theme in a safe and nurturing space designed to spark creativity and imagination, build confidence, and fill your kiddo's summer with music, crafts, sunshine, and memories!
