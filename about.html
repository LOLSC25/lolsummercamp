💛 Our Values

  • 🛡️ Safety always comes first — low counselor‑to‑camper ratios, allergy‑aware routines, and vigilant supervision keep every kiddo safe and having fun from drop‑off to pick‑up.
  • 🎨 Play with purpose — crafts, songs, and games that feel like pure fun while boosting fine‑motor skills, creativity, and social‑emotional smarts.
  • 💛 Kindness in action — “please,” “thank‑you,” and helping hands are woven into every activity so empathy becomes a habit, not a lecture.
  • 🌈 Inclusive joy — every child is welcomed exactly as they are, with activities adapted so each camper—reserved or daring—gets a chance to shine.
  • ✨ Faith‑inspired character — rooted in Christian love, we model gratitude, integrity, and compassion—values that resonate whether your family attends church or not.
