Our Daily Schedule

  • 🧸 Drop Off & Free Play – Campers ease into the day with open‑ended fun: building towers, pretending to cook, dressing up, and dancing it out.
  • 🎶 Circle Time – We clean up and come together for songs and silliness to kick off the day with big smiles.
  • 🎨 Craft Time – Simple, themed crafts that let little hands explore colors, glue, and glitter.
  • 🍎 Snack Time – A break to crunch, munch, and chat about the very important things in life (like dinosaurs and jellybeans).
  • 🌞 Outdoor Play – Running, climbing, chasing bubbles, making chalk art, and burning off our wiggles in the sunshine.
  • 🫧 Cleanup & Wash Up – We reset the space and scrub our paws—because lunchtime is just around the corner and our tummies are rumbling.
  • 🥪 Lunch – We laugh, eat, chat with friends, and try to solve the age‑old mystery: who did steal the cookie from the cookie jar?
  • ✨ Free Play & Pick Up – One last burst of imagination before heading home with tired feet and happy hearts.
