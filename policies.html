REGISTRATION

To start the registration process, please start by filling out our interest form.

Actual registration is done through a google sheet that you will be given access to after completing the registration form.

Registration closes at 1:00 PM (close of camp) on the Friday before the upcoming week of camp and will be locked at that time. If changes need to be made, please contact Lily to do so.

PAYMENT

Pricing: $30 a day or $135 for a full week (all 5 days).

We currently accept cash, check, or venmo.

Payment is due the Friday before the upcoming week at 1:00PM (close of camp). Failure to pay on time will result in your registration for that week being cancelled and you may be turned away at the door if you have not paid.

DROP OFF & PICKUP

Drop off begins at 8:55 AM every day and we will close the front door at 9:20 AM. If you are running late, please message us to let us know when to expect you. We will not accept late kiddos past 9:45 unless previous arrangement have been made.

Please pick up your kiddo promptly at 1:00 PM.
